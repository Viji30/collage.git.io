NATIONAL INSTITUTIONAL RANKING FRAMEWORK (NIRF)

The National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) is a
methodology adopted by the Ministry o Human Resource Development(MHRD),
Government o India, to rank institutions o higher education in India.The
framework was approved by the MHRD an launched by minister o Human Resource
Development on 29 september 2015.
